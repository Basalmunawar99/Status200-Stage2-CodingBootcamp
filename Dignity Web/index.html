Dignity for Children Foundation

Dignity for Children Foundation is a non-governmental organization that provides holistic care and education for marginalized and urban poor children in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Who are we?

Dignity for Children Foundation provides holistic care and education for urban poor children in Kuala Lumpur. Our work began in 1998, and we now support over 2,300 children. We utilize the Montessori method and Cambridge IGCSE curriculum, equipping underprivileged children with the skills they need to change their lives.

Our Services

We believe that education is transformational. Through our holistic educational approach, we empower students to become productive members of society, equipped with both academic and vocational skills.
